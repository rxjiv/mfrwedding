OUR STORY

During a global pandemic when masks were in and social distancing was the norm, our love story sparked with a Teams message. It didn’t take long to realize that no amount of quarantine could keep us apart.

From dating right away to meeting halfway in Prince Edward County for wine, sunsets, and responsible masking of course, we packed more milestones into eight months than some do in a lifetime. Engagement? Check. Courthouse wedding? Check. Moving to a whole new country together? Double check. Since then, we’ve been building a laughter-filled, slightly chaotic, and incredibly happy life together.

For years, it’s been an ongoing joke that our wedding was happening “next year”. Well, the wait is officially over! We’re beyond thrilled to celebrate our Hindu wedding and five-year anniversary with all of you in April 2026 in Mexico.

Thank you for being part of our love story—we can’t wait to eat, dance, and create unforgettable memories together!
